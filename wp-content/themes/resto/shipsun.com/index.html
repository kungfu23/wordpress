Our Mission

Our Mission is to be the preferred transportation service provider for businesses in California, Nevada and Arizona that demand efficient, reliable, competitively-priced services that our customers can count on to deliver consistently.

Keeping founder Hank Bachar’s vision of providing exceptional value to clients alive for over five decades.

Family owned and operated since 1954, Sun Express continues to raise the bar on transportation services, covering regions of California, Nevada and Arizona. Our clients depend on us to provide old fashioned service, always putting their needs and schedules first, which has earned us their trust and loyalty. ‘Going the extra mile’ is our commitment to you. We invite you to put Sun Express to the test for your next transportation need. You can count on us to deliver to your high expectations every time.
