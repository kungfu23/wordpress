About Us:

Going the extra mile.

Sun Express is a family-owned transportion company, serving California, Nevada and Arizona. Since 1954 we have been delivering consistent, reliable and courteous service to our clients, always at a fair price.

At Sun Express, we enjoy taking the burden of logistics off our clients to allow them to focus on growing their businesses. Our commitment to client satisfaction has helped us grow from a single delivery truck to a fleet of 50 trucks and 150 trailers! We work hard to meet or exceed the most demanding of expectations, because we know that if we do our job right, we’ll gain a client for life.

Many companies talk about ‘going the extra mile’. But at Sun Express, it’s our commitment to you.

Call us at 1-800-221-1480 and let us know how we can help with your transport needs. You’ll find friendly, knowledgeable staff members who will get you exactly the service you need, at a price you can afford.

History:

Keeping Hank’s vision alive.

It began with a truck and a vision. A Texan by birth, Hank Bachar moved to California in 1937, got married in 1950, started a family and settled in at Kaiser Steel. But Hank’s entrepreneurial spirit soon got him looking around for ways to make use of his talents and ambition. Identifying a growing need to provide more reliable transportation methods in the southland, Hank put his energy into launching his own trucking company, Hank’s Freight Service, in 1954.

It wasn’t long before Hank became known for going the extra mile for his clients. The fledgling business quickly grew into the highly successful business that ultimately became Sun Express.

While Hank has since passed on, his vision remains alive. The atmosphere Hank created to provide exceptional service and fair prices while nurturing long term customer relationships lives on everyday at Sun Express.
